Use of Cookies

The Kitty Kat Time website may use cookies to help personalize your online experience. A cookie is a small text file that is sent to your computer upon visiting a website. This packet of information is stored in your web browser. The purpose of a cookie is to help the website keep track of your visits and activity, and it can also help save you time. For example, a cookie helps the Kitty Kat Time website to retrieve your information, such as a billing address, for future visits. The information in a cookie does not get altered when it travels back and forth, so it cannot affect how your computer runs and cannot transfer viruses or malware to your computer.

Most web browsers are automatically setup to accept cookies, but you may choose to change your browser settings to decline cookies. If you decline cookies, you may not get the full interactive experience of the Kitty Kat Time services or other websites you visit.
