Nadia - The Owner


My name is Nadia, and I am the owner of Kitty Kat Time. Ever since I was little, animals have been my passion! I grew up with frogs, fish, and guinea pigs. I got my first cat when I was 6 and named her Snowflake. She was a gorgeous part Siamese/tabby cat. She experienced a lot in her 16 years on earth and even moved 3,000 miles across the country with me. Snowflake started my love for cats, and in high school I began volunteering at cat shelters. I was a cat care specialist volunteer for the Northwest Organization for Animal Help (NOAH) and volunteered with the Everett Animal Shelter as a cat room assistant. I got my Bachelor of Science degree in Animal Behavior and then became an independent contractor with two pet service companies, providing cat/small animal visits, overnight pet care, dog walking, and puppy training. I have experience with farm animals from working on a horse farm, interning at Farm Sanctuary’s Animal Acres, and volunteering at Pasado’s Safe Haven. I also volunteered at Sarvey Wildlife Care, helping to rehabilitate wild animals.
I currently have three cats named Salie, Popoki, and Dixie Rose.
