  • Whole-hearted cat sitting on Seattle's Eastside

Kitty Kat Time is a one-person owned and operated cat sitting business, providing in-home cat care while you're away, with an emphasis on your cat's health and wellbeing. I bring my own toys to initiate playtime and encourage exercise, which promote better sleep. I offer your cat healthy treats, like raw, freeze-dried treats and catnip. To help calm your cat, I provide music specially made for cats. I also provide any home care needed (getting the mail, watering plants, etc.) and send you text updates with pictures of your furry family member. I love getting to know every cat I meet and enjoy learning about his/her needs and unique personality. Hearing those satisfying purrs is music to my ears!

I am licensed, bonded, and insured and am certified in pet CPR and first aid.





My name is Nadia, and ever since I was little, animals have been my passion! I grew up with frogs, fish, and guinea pigs. I got my first cat when I was 6 and named her Snowflake. She was a gorgeous part Siamese/tabby cat. She experienced a lot in her 16 years on earth and even moved 3,000 miles across the country with me. Snowflake started my love for cats, and in high school I began volunteering at cat shelters. I was a cat care specialist volunteer for the Northwest Organization for Animal Help (NOAH) and volunteered with the Everett Animal Shelter as a cat room assistant. I got my Bachelor of Science degree in Animal Behavior and then became an independent contractor with two pet service companies, providing cat/small animal visits, overnight pet care, dog walking, and puppy training. I have experience with farm animals from working on a horse farm, interning at Farm Sanctuary’s Animal Acres, and volunteering at Pasado’s Safe Haven. I also volunteered at Sarvey Wildlife Care, helping to rehabilitate wild animals.
I currently have two cats named Popoki and Dixie Rose.
